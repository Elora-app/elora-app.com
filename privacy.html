Elora

by Rym

Privacy Policy · Last updated: June 2026

Elora is a private journaling and wellness app created by Rym. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. We take your privacy seriously — especially because Elora is a space for your most personal thoughts.

1. Who this applies to

This policy applies to all users of the Elora app, including users under the age of 18. If you are under 13, please do not use Elora without a parent or guardian's permission.

2. What we collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Your name and email address — collected when you create an account
  • Journal entries — the text, photos, and voice notes you write in the app
  • Mood logs — the moods you tap each day and any optional notes you add
  • Reflection answers — your responses to daily reflection questions
  • Letters to your future self — stored until your chosen delivery date
  • A PIN — A PIN — stored as a one-way hash (never readable by anyone, including us) to protect your account

Information collected automatically:

  • Basic usage data — which features you use, so we can improve the app
  • We do not collect advertising identifiers or sell data to third parties

3. How we use your data

We use your data to save and sync your journal entries, mood logs, and reflections across your devices, deliver your letter to future self on the date you choose, show you your mood history and insights within the app, and send you optional daily notifications if you allow them.

We do not sell your data, share your journal content with third parties, use your entries to train AI models, or show you ads.

4. Where your data is stored

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore database and Firebase Storage), hosted on Google's servers. Firebase is GDPR-compliant and uses industry-standard encryption. Photos and voice notes are stored in Firebase Storage, accessible only to your account.

5. Who can see your data

Only you. Your journal entries, mood logs, reflections, and voice notes are private by default and protected by your account login and your personal PIN. We — the app creators — do not read your entries. The only exception would be if we were legally required to disclose information by a court order, which we would only do if compelled by law.

6. Mental health data

Elora handles sensitive personal information including mood tracking, emotional reflections, and personal journaling. We treat this with the highest level of care. We will never use this data for advertising, profiling, or share it with insurance companies, employers, or any other third party.

If you are in crisis, please reach out to a mental health professional or crisis line. Elora is a journaling tool, not a substitute for professional support.

7. Children and teens

Elora is designed to be a safe space for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us at support@elora-app.com and we will delete the account promptly.

8. Your rights

You have the right to access your data, delete your data, export your journal entries, and withdraw consent at any time. If you are in the EU or UK, you also have rights under GDPR, including the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

9. Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your data within 30 days.

10. Notifications

If you allow notifications, Elora may send you daily check-in reminders and motivational messages. You can turn these off at any time in Settings → Notifications → Elora, or within the app under Profile → Notifications.

11. Third-party services

  • Google Firebase — database, authentication, and file storage
  • Apple — in-app purchases and app distribution
  • Expo — app development framework

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy as the app evolves. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app.

13. Contact us

support@elora-app.com
feedback@elora-app.com

We will respond within 5 business days.

Elora is built with care, for people who deserve a safe space. Your privacy is not an afterthought — it's the foundation.
